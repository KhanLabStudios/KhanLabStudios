About Timeframe
Welcome to Timeframe, an innovative multimedia studio from the First Year Humanities class! We develop and pitch captivating animated content that transforms historical subjects into entertaining stories. As 9th graders, we run the entire company, using our creativity and passion to make history both fun and informative. Our mission is to raise awareness, ignite interest, and engage in dialogue with the past.
